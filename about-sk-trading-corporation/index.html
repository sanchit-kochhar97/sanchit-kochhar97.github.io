Products

We deal 4 Major Segments

Tarpaulin: Tarpaulins, also known as tirpals, are strong and durable sheets of waterproof material that are used to protect objects from rain, dust

Shade Net: Used in agriculture, horticulture, and construction industries to protect crops, plants, and workers from excessive heat and UV radiation.

Tractor Hood Cloth: Tarpaulin that is specifically designed to cover and protect the engine and other parts of a tractor 

Mosquito Nets: Mosquito nets are meshed fabrics that are used to protect people from mosquito bites and other insect-borne diseases. They are commonly used in households & hospitals.
