About Us

SK Trading Corporation is a sole proprietor business that is involved in the manufacturing and wholesale distribution of a variety of products related to textiles and agriculture. Specifically, the company specializes in the production of green-net or shade netting, waterproof tripals, and tractor hood cloth. As a manufacturer, SK Trading Corporation is responsible for creating these products from raw materials. They may also be involved in designing the products and developing new prototypes. Once the products have been manufactured, SK Trading Corporation operates as a wholesaler, distributing their products to retailers or other businesses that will then sell the products directly to consumers. As a wholesaler, the company typically sells large quantities of their products at a discounted price, allowing them to reach a broader customer base and generate higher revenues. Overall, SK Trading Corporation's business activity can be described as a combination of manufacturing and wholesale distribution, with a focus on producing and selling textiles and agricultural products such as green-net or shade netting, waterproof tripals, and tractor hood cloth.
